At Prosec, We specialize in comprehensive cyber security servces, including pentesting, cloud security and Infra security. dedicated to protecting your digital assets and ensuring your business stays secure in an ever-evolving threat landscape.

Services

Penetration testing

Simulate real-world cyberattacks to uncover security gaps before malicious actors do. Our comprehensive penetration testing evaluates networks, applications, APIs, and endpoints to identify vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and potential exploits, providing actionable insights to strengthen your defenses

Cloud Assessment

Assess the security posture of your cloud environments — AWS, Azure, GCP, or hybrid — against industry standards and best practices. We identify misconfigurations, insecure access controls, and compliance risks, helping you harden your cloud infrastructure and safeguard critical assets.

Infrastructure Assessment

Evaluate the resilience of your internal and external IT infrastructure, including servers, databases, and network devices. Our infrastructure security assessment highlights weaknesses, recommends architecture improvements, and ensures your foundational systems are aligned with security benchmarks.

Source Code Analysis

Analyze application source code to detect security flaws like injection vulnerabilities, authentication issues, and insecure dependencies early in the development lifecycle. Our secure code review combines automated scanning and manual analysis to ensure your software is built with security in mind.

Container Image Analysis

Scan and audit container images for vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and compliance violations before they reach production.
